MATT TWORT

Across the past two years I have been lucky enough to have work of mine published on a range of online and print outlets.

My first regular gig with a major news outlet came in 2015 when I began writing Wellington club rugby reports for the Dominion Post.

Still, to this day, I am writing the same segment for the Dominion Post on a weekly basis between March and August, while keeping busy with a range of other works.

The now-defunct NZhoops website was my next calling. During my time there I reported on the New Zealand National Basketball League, mainly the Wellington Saints, as well as crafting a weekly wrap-up of Kiwi basketballers' performances in Europe.

That led to a position of Wellington Saints reporter for the New Zealand Herald in 2017 which entails me attending Saints games as accredited media, interviewing relevant parties during the post-match and writing match reports.

Other roles which I am currently a part of include weekly gambling analysis of the A-League for global football betting agency CubeGoal, as well as being a weekly contributor to ClubRugby.co.nz, writing feature articles, opinion pieces and match reports.

In future I will continue to write for my current employers, though when I graduate from university in November I will be looking for a full-time job. If that doesn't come about, I will likely look at doing Massey University's journalism postgraduate course.
