MATT TWORT
SPORTS JOURNALIST

I once had a dream of becoming an All Black. The next Hosea Gear (I loved him). Or, better yet, the next Matt Twort. I quickly realised that dream just wasn't going to happen. So, what next? Why not watch people throw around the oval ball, amongst other sports, and write about them? I guess that's where I'm at now.

First things first: I'm 20 years old, currently completing my third and final year of a Bachelor of Communications degree, majoring in Journalism, at Massey University, Wellington.

I have been free-lancing as a sports journalist for three years now, scoring my first regular gig at the age of 18, writing weekly club rugby reports for the Dominion Post, which I still do.

On top of that, I'm also the Wellington Saints reporter for the New Zealand Herald, a weekly contirbutor to rugby website Club Rugby, and an anylist for global gambling advice agency CubeGoal.

For examples of my work, please take a look at my portfolio where you can find direct links to the above-mentioned stories.

Lastly, thanks for taking the time to take a look at my website. Please do contact me for any queries or opportunities that you have.
